BOBBY IS STILL GOING HOME LEVELS CSV SCHEMA

    0 - stageNumber: game stage number
    1 - screenResource: screen resource number
    2 - songNumber: level song number
    3 - horizonSpriteNumber: horizon sprite number (0=nothing, 1=ship, 2=train)
    4 - horizonSpriteColor: horizon sprite color
    5 - skySpriteNumber: sky sprite number (0=nothing, 1=condor, 2=cloud, 3=eagle, 4=sun)
    6 - skySpriteColor: sky sprite color
    7 - bridgeFlag: 0=no bridge, 1=bridge
    8 - stationaryEnemyTile: stationary obstacle tile number (0=nothing, 1=hydrant, 2=flower, 3=fountain)
    9 - stationaryEnemyX: stationary obstacle X position
   10 - flyingEnemySpriteNumber: flying enemy sprite number (0=nothing, 1=bird, 2=butterfly, 3=bat)
   11 - flyingEnemySpriteColor: flying enemy sprite color
   12 - flyingEnemySpeed: 0=slowest, n=faster
   13 - rollingEnemyTile: rolling enemy tile (0=nothing, 1=barrel, 2=chicken)
   14 - rollingEnemySpeed: 0=slowest, n=faster
   15 - rollingEnemyQty: rolling enemy quantity on screen
