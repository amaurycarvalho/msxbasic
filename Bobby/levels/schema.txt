BOBBY IS STILL GOING HOME LEVELS CSV SCHEMA

    0 - stageNumber: game stage number
    1 - screenResource: screen resource number
    2 - songNumber: level song number
    3 - horizonSpriteNumber: horizon sprite number
    4 - horizonSpriteColor: horizon sprite color
    5 - skySpriteNumber: sky sprite number    
    6 - skySpriteColor: sky sprite color
    7 - bridgeFlag: 0=no bridge, 1=bridge
    8 - stationaryEnemyTile1: stationary obstacle tile 1 number
    9 - stationaryEnemyTile2: stationary obstacle tile 2 number
   10 - stationaryEnemyX: stationary obstacle X position
   11 - flyingEnemySpriteNumber: 0=no flying enemy, other=sprite number
   12 - flyingEnemySpriteColor: flying enemy sprite color
   13 - flyingEnemySpeed: 0=fastest, 1=fast, 2=slow, 3=slowest
   14 - rollingEnemyTile: 0=no rolling enemy, other=tile number
   15 - rollingEnemySpeed: 0=fastest, 1=fast, 2=slow, 3=slowest
   16 - rollingEnemyQty: rolling enemy quantity on screen
